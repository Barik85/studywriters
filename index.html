Reimbursement regulations

We offer competitive prices and base them on the following criteria:
Number of pages, Deadline Urgency, and Academic Level.
On the grounds of your performance, our QA team will award you a certain rating, which you must maintain for the long-run. Writer revaluations take place every 2 months, thus, you should be an active writer, and accept as many orders as you can.
The higher your rating, the better, and pricier, orders we will offer to you.
Payout schedule:

  • you will be paid for all the orders that have been accepted by our customers.
  • you request your accumulated salary amount during the payout window, which is from the 1-5th and 15-20th of each month. you are to be paid for every preceding 2 weeks.
  • we remit payments via PayPal only.

No place for tourists

StudyWriters.com - is a constantly growing academic writing agency, represented massively across the world to help students that struggle with their homework. Our primary principles are academic brilliance, integrity and loyalty in all levels of our work. Our writers are always taken care of, meaning there is always writing work, regardless of the time of the year. Our employment procedure is not extraordinarily rigid, but during the first weeks of work, which is considered as an internship period, writers are closely checked by the editors, and might lose their position due to unsatisfactory results. If you consider yourself a prominent writer and have knowledge of academic citation styles - do not hesitate to apply.
